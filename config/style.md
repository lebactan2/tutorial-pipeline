# Script Style Guide

## Voice and tone
- Clear, instructional, warm but not casual
- Use "let's" and "we" framing for tutorial steps
- Avoid jargon unless defining it immediately after

## Language handling
- Preserve the speaker's original language for each segment — never translate
- For mixed English/Vietnamese sentences, keep the mix intact
- Keep technical terms in English (e.g., "TouchDesigner", "workflow", "node")

## What to remove
- Filler: um, uh, so, like, you know, actually, basically, alright, ok, okay, all right, oh yeah, ừm, à, thì, kiểu, cái này, cái đó
- False starts: "I was going to — actually let me —"
- Repetitions of the same sentence
- Long silences (> 1.5s) between sentences
- Audible mistakes that the speaker corrected afterward

## What to keep
- Intentional pauses for emphasis (< 1.5s)
- Examples and asides that illustrate a point
- The speaker's natural phrasing and cadence
